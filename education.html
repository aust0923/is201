Education

Here is what I've learned.

I value education highly. I love to learn new things and better my life through the application of the things I learn. Nonetheless, I have somewhat of a love hate relationship with school. I have always received good grades. I received most every honors award you could receive throughout high school. I am a top performer in most of my university classes, yet, I have a burning desire to do more than sit in a classroom. My soul itches to get out in the world and learn more things that can't be taught in a classroom or by a book. I yearn to spend less time enclosed by a book, a school, a city, and more time learning about life through living it. All of that aside, though, I know that school is an important step in getting me to the places I want to be. For that reason, I have continued to immerse myself in traditional education and perform the best I can.
